Participant
    A participant is the person registering their party for the organizational event.

Party
    A party is a group of people who have registered together for an organizational event.

Org_event
    This is the organizational event (kayak trip, fundraiser, etc.). Parties attend organizational events.

Party_participant_list
    List of people in a particular party, identified by the registrant's email and the organizational event ID.

Org_event_party_list
    List of party id that are attending a particular organizational event (links parties to an event).

Participant_comments
    Any comments LICCB staff have about the registrant.
